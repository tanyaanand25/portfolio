Hi, I’m Tanya.

I’m a dancer and content creator passionate about expressing stories through movement and visual content. Over time, I have expanded my work into brand collaborations and photoshoots, helping businesses create authentic and engaging content for digital platforms.

My approach combines creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail. Whether it’s performing for the camera, creating dance-based content, or representing a brand through photography and video, I focus on delivering content that feels natural, energetic, and memorable.
